    How it works
    01 / INPUT
    Order data ingested
    Order details — product, customer history, delivery, payment method — are sent to the FastAPI backend running on Hugging Face.
    02 / ANALYZE
    AI risk matrix runs
    6 weighted dimensions are scored: customer history, delivery experience, product quality, payment method, pricing sensitivity, and review sentiment.
    03 / RESULT
    Risk score + action
    A 0–1 risk score is returned with a LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH label, top risk factors, and a recommended action for the operations team.
